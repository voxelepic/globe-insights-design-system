## Quick reference — Globe Insights

- **Aesthetic:** brutalism × 3D × eased-out parallax. Pure white background, 3px solid black borders, oversized black display type, single yellow accent (#FFE600).
- **Voice:** loud, direct, second-person, monospace body. Numbers as weapons. No travel cliches, no emoji.
- **Tokens:** see `colors_and_type.css` for colors, type, spacing, drop-blocks, motion easing.
- **UI kit:** `ui_kits/marketing/` is a working brutalist landing page. Copy components from `components.jsx` and styles from `styles.css`.
- **Substitutions flagged:** display face (Archivo Black/Space Grotesk) and icon set (Lucide CDN) are stand-ins — swap with licensed brand assets when available.
